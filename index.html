About Us

We are a South African agro-input company with decades of experience working with SA soils and climates. Our mission is sustainable, yield-driven farming that supports both commercial farms and smallholders across the country. We blend local knowledge with agronomic science to deliver formulations that work in practice, not just in theory.

Our approach starts in the soil — building structure, supplying key nutrients, and leveraging micronutrients to unlock plant health. We partner with regional agronomists and local farmers to test and refine products in real-world SA conditions, ensuring reliability from the field to the store.

We collaborate with distributors, farmer groups, and cooperatives to extend access, improve service levels, and share practical agronomy that translates to tangible results on the ground.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Which fertilizer is best for South African soils?
We tailor recommendations to your soil test results, crop, and local climate. Our soil-first formulations focus on achieving balanced nutrient availability and improved soil health for ongoing yields.
2. Do you offer soil testing or recommendations?
Yes. We partner with accredited agronomists to provide soil testing and local recommendations that inform crop-specific blends and soil conditioners.
3. Are your fertilizers suitable for smallholder farmers?
Absolutely. We design user-friendly blends and provide agronomic support to optimize input use, reduce waste, and improve soil health at scale.
4. Do you supply in bulk for commercial farms?
Yes. We have a reliable distribution network across SA and offer bulk options for commercial operations with consistent supply.
5. Are your products environmentally safe?
We prioritize environmentally responsible inputs, including organic and eco-friendly options, and ensure compliance with SA standards to minimize environmental impact.
6. How do I choose the right fertilizer for my crop?
Start with soil test results, then select crop-specific blends and soil conditioners designed for your soil profile and rainfall pattern. Our agronomists guide this process.
7. Do you deliver nationwide in South Africa?
We support a broad SA footprint with coordinated logistics to maximise reliability and minimize lead times for growers and distributors.
